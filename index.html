Our Vision

"Envisioning a world where the transformative power of education, environmental sustainability, and compassionate community support integrate together to uplift lives. We aspire to create a future where every individual has the tools to thrive, and hope transcends boundaries"



About Us

Beyond Pages Trust, founded in February 2024 by Mr. Nirmal Repakula, is dedicated to holistic community development through education, environmental sustainability, sports development, and humanitarian relief. Our vision is to integrate these elements to uplift lives globally, transcending boundaries with compassion and empowerment. We aim to foster hope and positive change by offering comprehensive assistance across various initiatives, ensuring every individual and community thrives sustainably. Upheld by values of integrity, empowerment, sustainability, compassion, and collaboration, we strive for lasting impact through tailored programs in education, environmental awareness, sportsmanship, humanitarian aid, food security, women empowerment, clothing donations, and rural development. Led by Mr. Nirmal Repakula's leadership and community-centered approach, Beyond Pages Trust stands out for its innovation, adaptability, and commitment to creating resilient communities and a healthier planet.

Our Services

At Beyond Pages Trust, we are dedicated to address various aspects of community development through a comprehensive range of programs, ensuring that no aspect of community development is overlooked.

Education

We provide educational resources and support to help individuals achieve their academic goals and unlock their potential. Our initiatives include scholarships, school supplies, computer skills enhancement, and after-school tutoring programs.

Environmental Sustainability

We promote sustainable practices and environmental awareness to protect and preserve our planet. Our projects include tree planting, waste management workshops, and community clean-up drives.

Sports Development

We encourage physical fitness and sportsmanship through various programs that foster teamwork and personal growth. Our activities range from organizing local sports events to providing training and resources for young athletes.

Humanitarian Relief

We assist communities in crisis, providing essential resources and support to help them rebuild and recover. Our relief efforts include disaster response, emergency aid distribution, and long-term recovery programs.

Medicine Distribution

At our NGO, we are dedicated to improving the health and well-being of under-privileged individuals by providing essential medical support. Our primary focus is on supplying life-saving medicines to diabetic patients on a regular basis. Through this initiative, we aim to provide necessary medications to manage their diabetes and lead healthier and quality lives.

Women Empowerment

We empower women through skill development programs, educational workshops, and support groups. Our initiatives aim to promote gender equality, enhance economic opportunities, and support women's health and well-being.

Clothing Donations

We collect and distribute old clothes to needy individuals and families, ensuring everyone has access to adequate clothing. Our clothing drives help provide warmth, dignity, and a sense of belonging to those we serve.

Rural Development

We focus on improving the quality of life in rural areas through infrastructure development, healthcare access, and agricultural support. Our projects aim to create sustainable and self-sufficient rural communities.

Why Volunteer with Us?

  • Make a Difference: Contribute to meaningful projects that have a direct impact on individuals and communities.
  • Learn and Grow: Gain new skills, knowledge, and experiences while working with a diverse group of people.
  • Connect and Network: Meet like-minded individuals, form lasting friendships, and expand your professional network.
  • Flexible Opportunities: Choose from a variety of volunteer roles that fit your schedule and interests.

Volunteer Opportunities

Tutoring and Mentoring

  • Help students of all ages improve their literacy and academic skills.
  • Provide guidance and support to individuals seeking personal and professional development.

Event Planning and Management

  • Assist in organizing and executing educational workshops, cultural events, and community outreach programs.
  • Engage with the community and promote our mission through various events.

Administrative Support

  • Support our team with administrative tasks, data entry, and office management.
  • Ensure the smooth operation of our programs and initiatives.

Creative and Technical Roles

  • Contribute your skills in graphic design, content creation, web development, and more.
  • Help us create engaging materials and maintain our online presence.

Community Outreach

  • Engage with local communities to raise awareness about our programs and services.
  • Build relationships and foster collaboration with community partners.

How to Get Involved

  1. Apply Online: Fill out our volunteer application form on the website.
